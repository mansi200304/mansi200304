### 📄 Conference Papers

**[JetMAE: How Colour Factors Explain 96% of a Neural Tagger's Decisions](https://openreview.net/attachment?id=nlt2r1P7yU&name=pdf)**
*Conference on Physics and AI at Stanford University (PAI26) / NeurIPS 2026*

> Introduced JETMAE — a masked autoencoder (MAE) treating jet physics observables as tokens, with MAE pre-training yielding AUC 0.8769 on 1M jets (+1.35% ± 0.28% over random init, p=0.014). Applied SHAP attribution and PySR symbolic regression to recover 95.9% of neural AUC in a single closed-form expression — directly applicable to **AI model interpretability and hallucination detection** in agentic security systems.
